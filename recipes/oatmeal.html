Oatmeal

Description

Oatmeal is a quick and easy breakfast option that is both nutritious and healthy. Follow this recipe to make the perfect meal to start your day!

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup of oatmeal
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 banana
  • 1/4 cup of blueberries
  • 3 strawberries

Steps

  1. Put oatmeal and water in a bowl. Let soak overnight.
  2. Slice up banana and strawberries. Add to the bowl.
  3. Add blueberries to the bowl.
  4. Serve.
