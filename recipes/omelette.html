Omelette

Description

Omelettes are a quick and easy breakfast option that is both nutritious and healthy. Follow this recipe to make the perfect meal to start your day!

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 a tomato
  • 1/2 an onion
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 2 strips of cooked bacon
  • 1/2 cup of spinach
  • 1/4 cup of cheese
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in a bowl.
  2. Dice all vegetables and add them to the bowl.
  3. Cut bacon into bit-sized pieces, add them to the bowl.
  4. Add cheese to the bowl.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Mix.
  7. Heat up a pan and add vegetable oil when hot. Add mixture.
  8. Flip omelette when bottom is cooked.
  9. Plate and serve.
